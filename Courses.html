Our Courses

Courses we Offer

VIT University offers a wide array of programs across its various campuses (Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal).

Under Graduation

An UG degree from VIT University can provide you with a strong academic foundation, practical skills, industry exposure, and good career opportunities, all within a well-reputed and globally recognized institution.

Post Graduation

A PG degree from VIT University can provide you with advanced knowledge, specialized skills, better career prospects, and opportunities in research, building upon the strong foundation of a reputable institution.

Research

VIT provides access to well-equipped laboratories and research centers. Each school often has specialized facilities relevant to its research domains.VIT has numerous collaborations and MoUs with both national and international universities and industries.

Our Facilities

VIT University offers a wide range of facilities to support the academic, research, and personal development of its students. .

World Class Library

The libraries offer a conducive environment for studying with reading halls and discussion rooms. They are centrally air-conditioned and Wi-Fi enabled.

Playground

VIT campuses provide a wide range of sports facilities, both indoor and outdoor, spread across a significant area.

Tasty and Healthy Food

VIT usually offers different types of messes to cater to diverse preferences, including South Indian Veg, South Indian Non-Veg, North Indian Veg, North Indian Non-Veg, and sometimes a "Special Mess" with a wider variety.
